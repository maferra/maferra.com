English Edition. Publication Date: October 4, 2019

This book is a collection of concise essays called tasks. These are my lessons learned, my cookbook of recipes to achieve outstanding results, or at a minimum, to build the proper mindset to achieve them. It's not a system, nor a bullet point list of different things to get done. It's my personal experience and view of the World, and a view that I believe can help anyone become the CEO of their own life.
